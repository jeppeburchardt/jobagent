MIND4iT is an agent representing a unique network of the strongest IT and business competences in the market.

MIND4iT is specialized in finding highly qualified consultants who can meet business development needs. We are one hundred percent focused on our customers' success and take pride in growing the relationships between clients and consultants based on trust, satisfaction and high professional insight. This means that we mediate external specialists who, at short notice, are able to step in with the necessary skills and qualities to perform all kinds of tasks - regardless of technology, platform, business sector or size.

MIND4iTs core services are:
- IT consultants on an hourly basis
- Management services
- Handling Services
- Try and hire services
- Recruitment of IT Professionals
